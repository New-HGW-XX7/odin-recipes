Spaghetti

Description

Everybody's go-to food when money is short. As it often is.

Ingredients

  • the cheapest spaghetti you can find - this is not a site for pasta connoisseurs
  • pre-made sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add some salt.
  2. Throw in the spaghetti.
  3. Wait. While waiting, heat up the sauce in a second pot or the microwave.
  4. Put pasta on a plate, pour over the sauce and get your stomach filled.
