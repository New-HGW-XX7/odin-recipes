Frozen Pizza

Description

You all know what a frozen pizza is. Above you can see my former all-time favourite. Unfortunately they changed something about the dough recently and now all of their products are inedible.

Don't be discouraged. There are plenty of other brands around you can choose from.

Ingredients

  • a frozen pizza of your choice
  • condiments - hot sauce, ketchup, mayo (please don't)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven according to the instructions on the packaging.
  2. Put the pizza in.
  3. Wait.
  4. When ready, taket it out. Don't let it cool off for too long.
  5. Enjoy.
